COMMUNICATION BEYOND WORDS

Communication is mode of transferring information to another. As social beings , communication is inevitable in our daily lives.
This could be through many forms,verbal communication is the most common all.

But its well known that words only go so far . which is where other modes and levels of communication take over the game :)

1) Communication beyond
LANGUAGE


Verbal communication using language is something understood by only few.
But there is one such breaks the barriers of language which is none other than . . ART

ART FORMS:-

Art is a special tool that connects people beyond the boundaries of creed , origin and language.

EXAMPLES:-

    (unlike verbal communication , which requires one to know a language)
  • A Painting's beauty communicates beyond language .

  • A Sportsman's team-spirit can communicate among a mix of players from all across earth.  

    (They need not know the language to play a team sport , sports have been known to bring peace between rival countries).

    • When global leaders are unable to communicate peace , when tension rises ,a good game played between the countries does a hidden job which we're unable to appreciate.

    When the countries are seen to switch between winning and loosing the game , a trial war has already happened. ut in this war there is only peace at the end.

  • Comedy or Acting out an emotion reaches to places where "WORDS" dared to go .

    Charlie Chaplin , Rowan Atkinson aka (Mr Bean) have been live examples in entertaining a pan audience , which was almost an immpossible task until then. As they saying goes :"Its always impossible until its done".

    Don't forget the fact that these two actors barely spoke in their entire acting career. Again the art of acting is prolific over words.


    

2) Communication beyond
SPECIES


This level of communication is that which goes even beyond language. . It is between two different species completely different in their physical and mental form
Even something as primordial as ART fails to reach this inaccessible location.
This is accomplished by something very simple , as well as special. Here the aspect of CARING comes to picture.

CARING:-

To devout oneself to another's wellbeing in any form possible.

MODES :-

  • Music if you thought was just an art form , you're mistaken
    The essence of music can be felt by anything with life. .
    Music has shown to improve plant growth and even influence animal behavior in many unexpected positive ways

    It has unexplainable significance towards the aspect of caring because its not just known , but confirmed by its ability to heal .  
    If you assumed it heals only the mental strata of a living object , you're once again mistaken as it has accelerated even physical healing :)

    Thy affirming the "Caring aspect" in the process of well-wishing that which is living (may it be plants seeing a jumped growth , or a fruit ripening quick , or a buffalo producing more milk , or a person healing from miseries )





  • FOOD  and FEEDING

    This shares Equal or more Credit towards the aspect of care compared to music .
    They say a dog is a man's best friend, but i'd like to say a dog is its feeder's best friend . A dog is loyal to who feeds it,not just a dog but sometimes even a hungry lion has reverence to the zookeeper who feeds it .

  • The action of providing a Meal is the highest level of service or care one can do to another.
    Though you may think providing food is a one way transaction . .
    It isn't :)




  • Those you made happy by providing food shall ever remain your well-wisher almost without them even realising it. I guess you know what happens after that
    As the author of "Alchemist" says :
    "When you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it " So somebody blessing you for your service,is a part of the universe already on your side :)

    Such things are incomprehensible to those who trust just the words from a mouth .

3) Communication beyond
ILLUSION


This is a level that's only visible with persistence effort to communicate Not just that it's also the most essential form , every other level is merely superficial Its not very astonishing upon slight analysis that the greatest , most underrated but most essential level of communication is possible only through SILENCE Yes you heard it right . .

SILENCE :-

To listen . . .

INSTANCES :

  • Amidst all chaos , there is a voice that always tries to communicate where we mostly deny it's chance. Its the voice within .  .
    Just minutes of unbiased observation of the voice within gives the direction of definite , away from addictions and temporary state of disguised pleasure which our brain enjoys on a regular basis.
    It also reveals to the listener the ignorance that was present to listen to it





  • To allow the voice to speak , one has to listen to it For one to listen , one has to remain silent and observe.
    Once there is clear communication by silence within oneself , every other layer and level of communication is a cakewalk .

    A simple level of miscommunication because of the momentary illusions can and has put an end to many relationships.
    These illusions test a man throughout life,to see his true potential and learning from an event.
    At the same time if one keeps all illusions away and takes a vulnerable position to get to good terms by speaking from within,that even if a failed attempt may seem the right thing to have done since one listened to oneself.
    As the wise saying goes
    "A man may win all arguements to keep his pride at high, but in the end he looses to himself"




    As i mentioned earlier,you need to listen . .
    but to whom ? is something one should ask oneself.
    People say there are so many question that do not have an answer.

    I must say "There are so many answers which haven't been asked the right questions" Silence helps one observe oneself,it reveals to a person the source of influence of his/her actions.

    Actions are happening and so are the results of actions,but due to noise from the external,the clarity of the superior call from inside diminishes.

    One forgets the source or influence of one's actions.

    Mind me that it isn't wrong to listen to others and follow them your entire journey,but also remember that your actions are now the same as a puppet.
    Your controllers as any influence but you.

    To be silent is to listen,to listen is to become aware of oneself.
    An analogy could be the sun's silent but powerful presence.
    Like a crystal under a X-ray study or a black-body radiation or just a simple mirror, they reflect/radiate only what shines upon it.
    Similarly, what you radiate or reflect is exactly what's within.
    Its finally time to clear the trash that's clogged :)

    Let this be your most prominet level of communication.
    All illusions soon disappear and the pieces of the puzzle are in a hand's reach once you master this.
